About Me

Data Analyst and Front-end Developer from Edinburgh, Scotland

It has taken me a long time to find the tech industry but I am so glad I have.

My Portfolio

Since graduating with a BSc (hons) in Business Studies and Psychology in 2010, I have been lucky enough to travel a lot of the world whilst working in a number of interesting roles, volunteering, exploring, and learning as I go. Working as a driver guide in Scotland upon my return kept my curious and adventurous mind busy - it was wonderful to share my knowledge with passengers from every country of the world as I led them around my own.

When the COVID-19 pandemic hit, I worked in a care home, completed a masters at the University of Edinburgh in Psychology of Mental Health, achieving a distinction, and joined taster sessions in software development. This sparked my excitement for the tech industry and I enrolled in SheCodes workshops where we mostly work with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After speaking with friends in the industry however, I also discovered an interest for data analysis.

I have now completed an incredibly interesting (and fun!) 14 week intensive course in data analysis with CodeClan in Edinburgh, Scotland. Whilst there, I worked mostly in RStudio whilst learning to clean datasets, analyse them in a number of ways, and present our findings on dashboards and during presentations. I also gained experience of working with SQL and Python and am excited for what the future may hold for me.
